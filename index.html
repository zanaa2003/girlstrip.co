Dabong

Dabong, a hidden gem in Kelantan, Malaysia, beckons with not only its natural wonders but also unique attractions like Gua Ikan, Stong Hill Resort, and Jelawang Pipe Resort. Venture into the depths of Gua Ikan, a captivating cave with intricate formations, offering a spelunking adventure that reveals the mysteries of the underground world. Strong Hill Resort provides a cozy retreat amidst the lush surroundings, offering breathtaking views of the Kelantan River and the Taman Negara rainforest. For a distinctive experience, Jelawang Pipe Resort combines luxury with nature, allowing you to unwind in stylish accommodation while being surrounded by the soothing sounds of flowing water from its ingenious water pipe setup. Join me on this vlog as we delve into the unique attractions that make Dabong a diverse and unforgettable destination!Interesting places in Dabong to explore

Gua Ikan

Gua Ikan, which means Fish Cave, is thought to be so-named because a stream flows inside, which may be home to fish.Well, there could be another theory. When approaching on highway 66 from the direction of Dabong, you will notice that the limestone hill structure containing the caves appears to be fish-shaped

4.5 Rating
Jelawang Pipe Resort and Extreme Park

Jelawang Pipe Resort and Extreme Park emerges as a captivating tourist haven in the picturesque state of Kelantan, nestled specifically within the enchanting Dabong district of Kuala Krai. Unveiled in 2018, this recreational oasis not only beckons adventure seekers but also boasts a myriad of exhilarating extreme sports activities, including invigorating hikes, heart-pounding flying fox experiences, and thrilling ATV rides. Beyond the pulse-quickening adventures, the resort offers luxurious accommodation available for rent, ensuring an unforgettable escape into the heart of nature's wonders.

5.0 Rating
Stong Hill Resort

o One of the highest waterfalls in Southeast Asia, and stay at Stong Hill Resort, the only hill resort in Mount Stong State Park, Dabong, Kelantan, Malaysia. Located in the foothills of the Mount Stong (i.e. one of the highest mountains in Kelantan), Stong Hill Resort provides accommodation with nine (9) chalets, three (3) dormitories, camping area, and a multi-purpose hall (Dewan Seraya hall), as well as other services such as hiking and cave exploration packages.
